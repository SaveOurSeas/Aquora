Smart Water Monitoring for a Cleaner Future

Aquora is an autonomous system that analyzes water quality in real-time using pH and turbidity sensors.

The Problem

Water pollution disrupts ecosystems, reduces oxygen levels, and threatens marine biodiversity worldwide.

Our System

Aquora Unit

Collects real-time pH and turbidity data from water sources.

Data Processing

Analyzes water quality and determines environmental risk levels.

Aqua Robot Link

Sends processed data to the Aqua robot for automated response.
