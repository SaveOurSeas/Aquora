Aquora

Aquora is a manually controlled water inspection robot designed for real environments. It moves through physical spaces using Bluetooth control and delivers live water readings directly to the operator through AquaTalkie — without automation or assumptions.

Manual Control

Full Bluetooth control over movement — operator stays in charge at all times.

Live Data Flow

Real-time sensor readings sent instantly during movement through the system.

Field Operation

Designed for real-world environments like farms, tanks, and testing zones.

System Purpose

“A direct link between physical movement and live environmental data.”

“Built for inspection where fixed sensors are not enough.”
