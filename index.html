Aquora
A dual-agent aquatic intelligence system designed to convert environmental water conditions into actionable digital insight through autonomous robotics and real-time analysis.
Not a single robot.
Not a sensor kit.

A distributed environmental intelligence network.
System Architecture

Scout Unit

The field intelligence layer. Deployed directly into aquatic environments to collect real-time pH and turbidity data.

Role: Environmental sensing agent

AquaTalkie Station

The analytical core. Receives, processes, and visualizes incoming environmental data streams.

Role: Data interpretation hub

Flow: Scout → Environment → Data Capture → Return → AquaTalkie Processing → Visualization
